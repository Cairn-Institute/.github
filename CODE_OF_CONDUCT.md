## Code of Conduct

CAIRN Institute is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in our technical community, including staff, contractors, students, interns, and open-source contributors.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully and constructively, welcome differing viewpoints and experience levels, focus feedback on the work rather than the person, and support a safe environment for students and newcomers.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language or imagery, personal attacks, sustained disruption of discussions, and sharing others' private information without consent are not tolerated in any CAIRN Institute space, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and code review.

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the CAIRN Institute Technical Owner or another designated organization contact. Reports will be handled discreetly.

## Enforcement

Organization Owners and Technical Leads may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary suspension, or removal from the organization, in response to violations of this code of conduct.
